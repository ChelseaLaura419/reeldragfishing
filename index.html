ABOUT US

Located on Florida’s east coast in New Smyrna Beach – Reel Drag Fishing Co. is owned and operated by husband and wife team, Matthew and Chelsea Wallenquest. Together, with their dedicated, seasoned crew and shallow water skiffs, Matthew and Chelsea have created some of the very best back water fishing charters in Florida.
